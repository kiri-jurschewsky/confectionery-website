About Us

We take pride in making our chocolate in our facilities with love and care. Our family has been making chocolate treats for generations, and it was just too good to keep to ourselves.

We have our own chocolate farm in Colombia where we have partnered with a small village to help us grow the cocoa to its full potential.

We hope you love our products just as much as we loved making them for you.
